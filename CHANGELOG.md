  * Slovak (sk-SK). _Thanks @lyallemma_.
  * German (de-DE). _Thanks @SvA1 and @Senuros_.
  * French (fr-FR). _Thanks @Xav83_.

  * Bangla (bn-BD). _Thanks @sarequl_.
  * Italian (it-IT). _Thanks @Pyr0x1_.

* Using MASM x64 version.
